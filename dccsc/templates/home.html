About Our Club

Welcome to the Dhaka City College Science Club, exclusively for the HSC27 Batch. We're not just a club - we're a community of innovators, thinkers, and creators pushing the boundaries of science and technology.

Our mission is to foster scientific curiosity, critical thinking, and hands-on learning through experiments, projects, and collaborations that make science exciting and accessible.

From robotics to biochemistry, from astronomy to environmental science - we explore it all with passion and purpose.
